EEex_MemorizeInnateSpell 
===================================

::

   EEex_MemorizeInnateSpell(actorID, resref)

Memorize an innate spell for an actor

**Parameters**

* **actorID** - id of actor that is memorizing spell
* **resref** - resource reference of spell to memorize

**Return**

None

**Example**

   EEex_MemorizeInnateSpell()
